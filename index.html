Junior Program

The Junior Program of our hockey club is designed to nurture young talent and foster a love for the sport. This program offers comprehensive training sessions led by experienced coaches who focus on developing fundamental skills and techniques.

Teenager Program

Our Teenager Program is designed to develop young athletes' skills, teamwork, and passion for hockey. Participants will engage in intensive training sessions led by experienced coaches, focusing on both individual skills and team strategies.

Professional Program

The Professional Program of our hockey club is designed for athletes seeking to elevate their game to the highest level. This program offers intensive training sessions led by experienced coaches with professional playing backgrounds.
